CATEGORIES

Our pricing is value-based and tailored to the complexity of your specific requirements. We typically work with a project-based fee for initial builds and offer flexible retainer models for ongoing support. Every proposal includes a detailed breakdown of costs, from design exploration to final deployment, ensuring complete transparency with no hidden fees.

A typical high-end project spans between 8 to 14 weeks. This timeline allows for our rigorous craftsmanship process: 2 weeks for discovery and strategy, 4 weeks for deep UI/UX design, and 4-6 weeks for technical development and performance optimization. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure your digital product stands out as a piece of art.

We include three major revision rounds within our standard contract. However, our process is highly collaborative. We present work in "sprints" to get your feedback early and often, which usually minimizes the need for drastic changes during the final stages. Our goal is absolute perfection and client satisfaction.

We utilize a modern, high-performance tech stack including React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS for the frontend, often backed by headless CMS solutions like Sanity or Strapi. For complex integrations, we leverage Node.js or Python. We select the technology based on your project's specific performance and scalability needs.

Yes, we offer comprehensive "Concierge Support" packages. This includes hosting management, security updates, performance monitoring, and ongoing design/content updates. We believe a digital product is a living entity that needs consistent care to maintain its premium status and technical edge.
